# Final Project (Updated) Plan

Bill of Materials:

 * 3 lbs of 20AWG Magnet wire (1000' = 3lbs)
 * 48 count bar magnets
 * 1 kg ASA Filament
 * 16 count small magnets
 * 1 count Hall effect sensor
 * epoxy
 * ~20-30 count MOSFETS IRF3205
